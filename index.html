about me

I am a software engineer and security researcher, currently a researcher in the Secure Software Engineering (SSE) research group at TU Clausthal, Germany. My research interests focus on mining software repositories for security applications. I hold an M.Sc. in Information Technology Engineering with a specialization in Secure Computing from Shiraz University, obtained in 2020.
